Crystal Collector

Game Objective:

  • Match your total crystal points to the given goal number.


How It Works:

  1. The game starts with a random generation of "Goal Number". This is your target.
  2. Observe the four crystals displayed below:
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Click a crystal to add a specific number of points to your total score.
  4. You win by matching your points to the goal number exactly.
  5. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.


Things To Keep In Mind:

  • The crystal value is hidden while playing, so remember to note each one's points on your own.
  • You lose if your total points exceed the goal number.
  • When a round ends (either winning or losing), the game will reset. A new goal/crystal point value will generate, and your score will be tracked in the "Wins/Losses" table.
  • DON'T REFRESH THE PAGE. You'll lose your win/lose score.
